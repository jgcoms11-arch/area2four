FAQ

Questions We Hear a Lot

Here's what most customers want to know before booking.

Most standard installs take 1–3 hours depending on the number of windows and complexity of the vehicle. Full vehicle tints or ceramic installs may take slightly longer. We'll give you a realistic time estimate when you book your appointment.

Standard tint uses dye or metalized film that reduces light and adds privacy but has limited heat rejection over time. Ceramic tint uses nano-ceramic particles that block significantly more heat and UV — without going darker and without interfering with GPS, Bluetooth, or cell signals. It's the premium option for performance.

It depends on your goals, your vehicle, and your state's window tint laws. Common options range from 5% (very dark, limo-style) to 50% (light, barely visible). We'll help you understand your state's legal limits and find the right shade for your situation during your quote or consultation.

Yes. We offer tint removal for old, bubbling, faded, or damaged film. We carefully peel back the existing tint and clean the glass so it's completely prepped — whether you're getting a fresh install or just want clean windows.

After your install, avoid rolling your windows down for a few days while the film cures. When cleaning the inside of your windows, skip ammonia-based products and use a soft cloth with a mild cleaner. We'll go over all the care details when you pick up your vehicle.

Fill out the quote form below, call or text us, or send us a DM on Instagram @area2four. Just tell us your vehicle year, make, and model and what you're looking for — we'll get back to you quickly.

Tint laws vary by state and sometimes by vehicle type. We know the regulations in our area and will make sure your install is within the legal limits for the windows being tinted. When in doubt, just ask us — we'll point you in the right direction.
